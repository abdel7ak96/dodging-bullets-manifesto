The values I strive to embody

I'm an explorer.

Inquistiveness

The quality of being given to asking questions; intellectual curiosity or eagerness for knowledge.

Open-mindedness

Receptiveness to new ideas. Open-mindedness in the way in which I approach the views and knowledge of others.

Discipline

The quality of being able to behave and work in a controlled and systematic manner which involves setting rules and consistently abiding by them.

Rigour

The quality of carrying ones duties and tasks in a detailed, careful and thorough manner.

Conscientiousness

The tendency to take obligations to others very seriously and to go above and beyond to fulfill promises and expectations.

Persistance

The ability to continuously endure drudge work in spite of opposition, difficulty and above all in face of initial unsatisfactory results.

Ambition

Strong desire for advancement and reaching one’s full potential.

Adaptability

The ability and willingness to take the form of whatever conditions are required to achieve one’s goals.
