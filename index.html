The Dodging Bullets manifesto

AKA My Cover Letter

My mission is express my burning desire to make my mark in tech.

This entails giving my utmost effort to become part of the Spooniverse, a community where I can collaborate with similarly passionate and talented individuals, mutually elevating and inspiring one another.

I consider myself to be a vibrant, passionate, motivated, and exceedingly curious individual. Moreover, I am driven by a strong desire to make a meaningful impact.

And that means that I will LOVE it in the Spooniverse!

The values I strive to embody

I'm an explorer.

Inquistiveness

The quality of being given to asking questions; intellectual curiosity or eagerness for knowledge.

Open-mindedness

Receptiveness to new ideas. Open-mindedness in the way in which I approach the views and knowledge of others.

Discipline

The quality of being able to behave and work in a controlled and systematic manner which involves setting rules and consistently abiding by them.

Rigour

The quality of carrying ones duties and tasks in a detailed, careful and thorough manner.

Conscientiousness

The tendency to take obligations to others very seriously and to go above and beyond to fulfill promises and expectations.

Persistance

The ability to continuously endure drudge work in spite of opposition, difficulty and above all in face of initial unsatisfactory results.

Ambition

Strong desire for advancement and reaching one’s full potential.

Adaptability

The ability and willingness to take the form of whatever conditions are required to achieve one’s goals.

Examples of how I'm an explorer

I actively seek new knowledge and innovative solutions. Embracing curiosity, I ensure a continuous exploration of the unknown, staying at the forefront of industry developments.

Discipline defines my work ethic. Meeting deadlines consistently and delivering high-quality results, I contribute to a standard of excellence in both individual and team efforts.

I ensure precision and reliability in every task. Upholding a standard of rigour, I contribute to the creation of robust outcomes, fostering a workplace culture that values thoroughness.

In the face of challenges, persistence is a defining characteristic. Rather than succumbing to setbacks, I persevere through difficulties with determination and resilience.

I'm an engineer.

Creativity

The quality of generating original and imaginative ideas, fostering innovation and bringing fresh perspectives to problem-solving.

Communication

The art of effectively expressing ideas and information, ensuring clear and comprehensive understanding through attentive listening.

Collaboration

The ability to work seamlessly with others, leveraging diverse skills and fostering teamwork to achieve collective success.

Continuous learning

The commitment to ongoing technical development, actively seeking new knowledge for adaptability in a rapidly evolving technology landscape.

Resourcefulness

The quality of finding creative and effective solutions with available resources, demonstrating a practical and innovative approach to problem-solving.

Humility

The virtue of recognizing and acknowledging one's limitations, fostering a modest and respectful attitude and openness to feedback.

Humor

The ability to bring levity and joy into work and interactions, fostering positive relationships and creating an enjoyable and productive environment.

Passion

The intense enthusiasm and commitment towards one's work, driving individuals to go above and beyond and inspiring others through their fervor.

Examples of how I'm an engineer

I seamlessly work with team members, leveraging diverse skills to achieve collective success. I recognize the value of collective effort and positive team dynamics.

I navigate challenges by finding creative and effective solutions with the available resources. This practical and innovative approach enables me to optimize processes and overcome obstacles.

I acknowledge my limitations and welcome feedback. This modest and respectful attitude fosters a collaborative atmosphere, where learning from others and adapting based on constructive input.

I bring a positive and enjoyable atmosphere to the workplace. Recognizing the importance of levity, I allow humor to facilitate camarderie and enhance my team dynamics.

I belong

Tolerance

Proactivity

Self-initiated behavior that endeavours to solve a problem before it has occurred. Proactive behavior involves acting in advance of a future situation

Goodwill

The quality of carrying each task with the intention of making the lives of the people around easier and better.

Cultural Sensitivity

The knowledge, awareness, and acceptance of other cultures and others' cultural identities without casting judgments.

Cheerfulness

the quality or state of being noticeably happy and optimistic.

Examples of how I belong

By recognizing and acknowledging my collegues background, perspectives and feelings, I contribute to a supportive work environment built on understanding and well-being.

Rooted in goodwill, I cultivate positive relationships and a constructive attitude within the team.

I take the initiative to identify potential issues and contribute to dynamic and forward-thinking team culture, ensuring that we anticipate and address changes effectively for collective success.

I'm deeply curious about other cultures and I believe cultural diversity enriches workplaces.
